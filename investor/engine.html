FuFirE Engine

FuFirE is the product core, not a decorative label. It is the layer that turns precision astronomy and BaZi time logic into a single coherent signal that can be reused across the product.

What it does

The engine computes Western positions and BaZi pillars separately, then maps both into a shared five-element representation. This allows one coherence score, one distribution, and one product-ready interpretation layer instead of parallel systems that never really meet.

Why that matters

  • Most consumer apps simplify East-West fusion into a story device.
  • FuFirE treats fusion as a computation problem.
  • That creates a stronger foundation for trust, pricing, and later API packaging.
