# Stakeholders

Everyone with a stake in the system: those who use it, fund it, maintain it, or are affected by it. Every requirement should trace back to a stakeholder need.

## Influence Levels

- **High** — can approve or veto decisions; priority conflicts resolved in their favor
- **Medium** — consulted during review; concerns addressed but may be overruled
- **Low** — informed of decisions; needs considered but not blocking

## Persona Market Thesis

The four personas (Lena, Marc, Sophia, Kai) are not just user archetypes — they are **investor proof points** demonstrating that BAZODIAC's addressable market extends far beyond the average astrology-as-entertainment user (e.g., Co-Star).

**Target segment**: Agnostic, semi-spiritual, framework-curious users with higher income and education who use identity systems as **thinking tools for reflection and insight** — not as belief systems.

**Why this segment pays more**: They value mathematical transparency and intellectual depth. BAZODIAC functions as a thinking model that provides new perspectives on decisions, relationships, and life questions. The mathematics creates trust; the openness to spirituality creates willingness to engage.
