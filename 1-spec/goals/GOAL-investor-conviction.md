# GOAL-investor-conviction: Make Investors Emotionally and Intellectually Convinced

**Description**: The insight page at bazodiac.space must function as a self-contained pitch that moves a prospective investor from curiosity to conviction within a single scroll session. It must answer the three core investor questions — "Is this a real market?", "Can this team build it?", and "Why now?" — without requiring a separate deck, call, or document. The page is the first (and possibly only) touchpoint before a meeting request.

**Status**: Draft

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-founder](../stakeholders.md), [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

## Success Criteria

- [ ] An investor viewing the page for the first time can articulate what BAZODIAC does, why the market exists, and what the ask is — within 3 minutes of landing
- [ ] The page generates at least one qualified investor inquiry (email or meeting request) per 20 unique investor-profile visits
- [ ] Every market claim on the page links to or is derivable from a verifiable source (no unattributed statistics)
- [ ] The page includes a clear, low-friction call-to-action for investors (early access request, meeting booking, or contact form)
- [ ] The narrative arc follows problem → product → proof → opportunity → ask without requiring external context
