# REQ-F-segment-contrast-display: Visual Comparison of Target Segment vs. Entertainment Astrology

**Type**: Functional

**Status**: Draft

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source**: [US-investor-segment-contrast](../user-stories/US-investor-segment-contrast.md)

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

## Description

The page must include an explicit visual comparison between BAZODIAC's target segment (agnostic, framework-curious, higher income/education) and the entertainment-astrology segment (Co-Star's core user). The comparison must show: different motivations (thinking tool vs. entertainment), different willingness to pay (subscription vs. free/$0 ARPU), and different retention drivers (mathematical trust vs. content novelty). Must include at least 2 external data points supporting the premium segment's existence.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given the segment contrast is visible, when an investor reads it, then two columns or rows clearly distinguish "Entertainment Astrology" from "Framework-Curious Identity Tools"
- Given the contrast, when checking for evidence, then at least 2 external benchmarks are cited (e.g., MBTI pricing $49.95, Headspace €69.99/year, Enneagram coaching $100–200/session)
- Given the positioning statement, when an investor reads it, then the page communicates: "The mathematics creates trust; the openness to spirituality creates willingness to engage"
