# REQ-F-risk-section-content: New Page Section Presenting Both Critical Risks

**Type**: Functional

**Status**: Draft

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source**: [US-investor-sees-risk-framework](../user-stories/US-investor-sees-risk-framework.md)

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

## Description

The page must include a new section presenting both critical risks with honest diagnosis and strategic response. For each risk: (1) risk name and one-sentence diagnosis of why it's real, (2) strategic defense lines as summary bullets (not full detail — keep it scannable), (3) visual indicator of severity (e.g., ⚠️ HIGH). The section must convey risk awareness without overwhelming the page flow — it should feel confident, not anxious.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given the risk section exists, when an investor reads it, then both risks are named: "Substance vs. UX" and "Premium Willingness to Pay"
- Given each risk, when reading the diagnosis, then 1-2 sentences explain why the risk is real (not dismissed)
- Given each risk, when reading the defense, then 2-3 bullet points summarize the strategic response (Proof of Precision, Companion-First, FaaS fallback for Risk 1; Tiered validation, DACH pricing, early warning signals for Risk 2)
- Given the section design, when comparing to other sections, then it uses the same glass-card styling and design language — not a different visual tone
