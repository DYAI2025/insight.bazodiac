# ASM-preliminary-pricing: Current €4.99/month Price Point is Placeholder

**Category**: Business

**Status**: Unverified

**Risk if wrong**: Medium — if the preliminary price is presented as final to investors, it undermines credibility. Investors expect pricing to be derived from unit economics, not assumed. Conversely, if the price point turns out to be too low after analysis, it sets wrong expectations with early users.

## Statement

The €4.99/month subscription price is a **preliminary benchmark** used for illustration purposes only. It is assumed that the actual price will be determined through investor collaboration, break-even analysis, and willingness-to-pay research across persona segments.

## Rationale

- No cost coverage analysis has been performed yet
- No break-even calculation exists for infrastructure, content generation, or API costs
- No real willingness-to-pay data has been collected from the target segments
- Competitor pricing (Co-Star: free/ad-supported; premium identity tools: $10–50/month) suggests a wide range is viable
- The premium positioning (higher income/education segment, "Dark Luxury" brand) may support a significantly higher price point than €4.99

## Verification Plan

1. Conduct willingness-to-pay survey across Lena/Marc/Sophia/Kai segments (pre-launch beta)
2. Model unit economics: CAC per persona × required LTV multiplier → minimum viable subscription price
3. Validate with investor input during funding discussions
4. A/B test pricing tiers during soft launch
