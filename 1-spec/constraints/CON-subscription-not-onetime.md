# CON-subscription-not-onetime: Monetization is Subscription, Not One-Time Payment

**Category**: Business

**Status**: Active

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-founder](../stakeholders.md)

## Description

BAZODIAC's monetization model is a **monthly subscription** (currently benchmarked at €4.99/month for full premium access). All references to a one-time payment of €4.99 are the result of an early translation error and must be corrected wherever they appear.

The €4.99/month price point is **preliminary and illustrative only**. It is not based on cost coverage analysis, break-even calculation, or real user data. Final pricing must be developed in collaboration with investors based on:

- Actual unit economics (CAC, LTV per persona segment)
- Break-even analysis against infrastructure and content costs
- Competitive positioning against identity-framework tools (not astrology apps)
- Willingness-to-pay research across the four persona segments

## Rationale

A subscription model aligns with the product's value proposition:

1. **Daily engagement mechanics** (Morning Mail transits, Daily Signature Resonance) create recurring value that justifies recurring payment
2. **Transit data is time-dependent** — the Signal changes daily, making one-time access structurally incomplete
3. **Retention-driven personas** (Sophia, Kai) require ongoing content delivery to sustain engagement
4. **LTV optimization** — subscription compounds revenue per user over time, critical for the high-LTV segments (Marc, Lena)
5. **Investor alignment** — recurring revenue is the standard metric for SaaS/subscription valuation

## Impact

- All investor-facing materials (including insight.bazodiac) must present subscription pricing, not one-time
- Any legacy references to "€4.99 one-time" or "One-Time-Payment" must be identified and corrected
- Pricing section on the insight page should clearly mark current figures as preliminary ("starting at" / "from" language)
- Final pricing is a **joint founder-investor decision** — not to be hardcoded until validated
