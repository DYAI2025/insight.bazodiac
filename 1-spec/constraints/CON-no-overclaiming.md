# CON-no-overclaiming: No Unverified Superlatives or Mythopoetic Language in Investor Materials

**Category**: Business

**Status**: Active

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-founder](../stakeholders.md), [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md), [STK-marc](../stakeholders.md)

## Description

A critical competitive analysis identified that FuFirE currently suffers from **Overconfidence Bias combined with Complexity Bias**. The insight page must not perpetuate this.

### Prohibited Patterns

**Unverified superlatives** — the following claims (or equivalents) must NOT appear without hard evidence:
- "Only engine worldwide"
- "Technological market leader"
- "World's first" (unless verifiably true with dated prior art search)
- "Data Moat" (unless the dataset exists and is quantified)
- "Mathematically valid synthesis" as a global uniqueness claim (without peer review or published methodology)

**Mythopoetic marketing language in technical contexts** — the following terms mix hard computation claims with symbolic elevation, reducing perceived credibility for analytically minded investors:
- "Obsidian Core" → use "Deterministic Calculation Layer"
- "Neural Mycelium" → use "Modulation Layer" or "Adaptive Context Layer"
- "Bioluminescent Membrane" → use "Visualization Layer" or "UI Rendering Layer"
- "Soul Signature" → use "Personal Signal" or "Identity Signal"
- "Digital Organism" → use "Three-Layer Architecture"

**Note**: These terms may be appropriate in consumer-facing brand materials (Lena, Kai personas). They are **not appropriate** on the investor insight page, where credibility depends on precision and sobriety.

### Required Pattern

Every claim on the insight page must follow one of:
1. **Verified** — linked to a named source (research report, published data, app store analysis)
2. **Derivable** — explained with a clear derivation method that an investor can follow
3. **Labeled as preliminary** — explicitly marked as estimate, projection, or assumption

### The Core Insight

> "The product sometimes acts as if complexity itself should count as evidence. That is dangerous for trust."
> — Competitive analysis, critical assessment of FuFirE

The insight page must demonstrate that BAZODIAC understands this trap and has corrected for it.

## Rationale

The competitive analysis concluded:
- Cosmic Fusion wins on **perceived credibility** for the general market due to simpler, more understandable product logic
- FuFirE wins on **substance potential** but only if claims are demythologized and empirically verified
- For due-diligence investors who evaluate architecture and model logic, FuFirE has greater substance — but the current rhetoric undermines this advantage

An investor who encounters overclaiming will discount everything else on the page, including the genuinely strong mathematical foundations.

## Impact

- Full text audit of `index.html` required to identify and replace prohibited patterns
- Technical descriptions must use precise, sober engineering language
- Market claims must include source citations
- "Blue Ocean" framing must be adjusted per [CON-cosmic-fusion-incumbent](CON-cosmic-fusion-incumbent.md)
