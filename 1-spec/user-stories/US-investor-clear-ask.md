# US-investor-clear-ask: Find a Clear Call-to-Action Without Searching

**As a** prospective investor, **I want** a clear, low-friction next step (meeting request, email, or early access form) that I can find without scrolling through the entire page, **so that** I can act on my interest immediately when conviction forms — whether at the top, middle, or bottom.

**Status**: Draft

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

**Related goal**: [GOAL-investor-conviction](../goals/GOAL-investor-conviction.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given I am convinced early (after hero + problem section), when I look for a CTA, then I find an investor-specific link or button within the first viewport or navigation (e.g., "For Investors →")
- Given I scroll the entire page, when I reach the final section, then there is a prominent CTA with an email input or meeting booking link
- Given I am on mobile, when I want to act, then the CTA is reachable without excessive scrolling and the input fields are thumb-friendly
- Given I submit my email or request, when the form processes, then I receive clear confirmation that my inquiry was received (not a silent submit)
