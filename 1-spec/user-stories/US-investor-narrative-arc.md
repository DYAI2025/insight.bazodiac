# US-investor-narrative-arc: Follow Problem → Product → Proof → Opportunity → Ask in One Scroll

**As a** prospective investor, **I want** the page to tell a coherent story from problem through solution to investment opportunity in a single scroll session, **so that** I build conviction progressively without needing to jump between sections or consult external documents.

**Status**: Draft

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

**Related goal**: [GOAL-investor-conviction](../goals/GOAL-investor-conviction.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given I start at the hero, when I scroll through the entire page, then the sections follow this narrative arc:
  1. **Hook** — tagline + confidential preview framing
  2. **Problem** — the illusion of depth in current astrology (one-dimensional, no math, text retrieval)
  3. **Product** — three-system fusion, FuFirE engine, the Signal formula
  4. **Proof** — personas proving market demand, competitive positioning vs. Cosmic Fusion
  5. **Opportunity** — market size (sourced), unit economics, growth mechanics
  6. **Risk awareness** — critical assumptions, defense lines, kill criteria
  7. **Ask** — clear CTA for investor next step
- Given I read each section, when I transition to the next, then the connection is clear — no section feels orphaned or out of sequence
- Given I want to skip to a specific section, when I look for navigation, then I find section anchors or a minimal nav that lets me jump (mobile nav dots or sticky header links)
