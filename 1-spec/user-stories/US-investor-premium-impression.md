# US-investor-premium-impression: First Impression Says "Deep Tech", Not "Astrology App"

**As a** prospective investor, **I want** my first visual impression of the page to evoke "fintech / deep-tech platform" before "astrology / horoscope app", **so that** I take the product seriously as a technology investment — not dismiss it as another wellness fad.

**Status**: Must-have

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md), [STK-lena](../stakeholders.md)

**Related goal**: [GOAL-premium-brand-perception](../goals/GOAL-premium-brand-perception.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given I open the page for the first time, when I see the hero section (before reading any text), then the visual design language (dark background, gold accents, particle physics visualization, monospace technical elements) signals technology and precision — not crystals, zodiac clip art, or pastel gradients
- Given I take a screenshot of any single section, when I show it to a colleague without context, then the most common first descriptor is "tech" or "data" rather than "astrology" or "horoscope"
- Given I am comparing mentally to other investment opportunities, when I assess the page's production quality, then it feels comparable to a Stripe, Linear, or Vercel-tier product page — not a template or WordPress site
- Given I have seen Cosmic Fusion's materials, when I compare visual quality, then BAZODIAC's insight page is unambiguously more sophisticated in design execution
