# US-investor-sees-personas: See Four Personas With Data-Backed Demographic Detail

**As a** prospective investor, **I want** to see the four target personas (Lena, Marc, Sophia, Kai) presented with enough demographic, behavioral, and economic detail that I understand who will use and pay for BAZODIAC, **so that** I can evaluate whether the target market is real, reachable, and monetizable.

**Status**: Must-have

**Priority**: Must-have

**Source stakeholder**: [STK-investor](../stakeholders.md)

**Related goal**: [GOAL-persona-market-proof](../goals/GOAL-persona-market-proof.md)

## Acceptance Criteria

- Given I reach the persona section, when I read each persona card, then I see: archetype name, age, profession, city/region, income range, relationship to BAZODIAC (why they use it — as thinking tool, not belief), and their monetization role (brand trust / revenue anchor / retention / acquisition)
- Given I am evaluating market size, when I look at the personas, then at least one data-backed claim about segment size or behavior is presented per persona (e.g., "73% of Gen Z use identity frameworks" — sourced)
- Given I want to understand the business model, when I see the persona matrix, then the four roles (brand standard-setter, revenue anchor, retention engine, acquisition funnel) are clearly mapped to the subscription tiers and revenue model
- Given I know the personas are not current users but proof points, when I read the section, then the framing is honest — they represent the target segment, not existing traction
