Architecture

Bazodiac is easiest to understand as a four-layer system: astronomical truth, symbolic fusion, product logic, and investor-facing delivery. Each layer narrows ambiguity instead of adding mystique.

What the stack really means

The top of the stack is not astrology content. It is high-quality input handling: civil time, timezone, place, and birth precision. The second layer is calculation. The third layer is fusion. The fourth is what an investor sees: usable outputs, repeatable product logic, and monetizable endpoints.
